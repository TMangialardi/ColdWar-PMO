Cold War: the roles

CIA

"Unseen, Unheard, Untouchable: CIA, the ultimate covert team!" The CIA is the foreign intelligence service of the federal government of the United States. Its goal in the game is to protect the United States from Soviet attacks. It wins the game if there are more eliminated KGB members than eliminated CIA members.

KGB

"Silent, Lethal, Unstoppable: KGB, the masters of espionage!" The KGB is the main security agency for the Soviet Union and conducts covert operations to advance Soviet interests. It wins the game if there are more eliminated CIA members than eliminated KGB members.

Double agents

"Betrayal is their game, but no one can predict their next move: Double agents, the ultimate wildcards!" Double agents infiltrate enemy intelligence agencies to gather information, manipulate operations, and sabotage missions. They win the game if there is a balance between the number of CIA and KGB members eliminated.
