Cold War: the missions

Murder

"Sometimes a secret agent has to do a dirty job" the player, regardless of his team, must cause another player to be eliminated, regardless of that player's team. If the mission is completed within the entire match, the player wins.

Suicide

"A secret agent must do whatever it takes to defend its mission" the player, regardless of his team, must get eliminated by the other players. If the mission is completed within the entire match, the player wins.

Bodyguard

"Protect with honor, serve with pride: Be the ultimate Bodyguard!" the player, regardless of his team, must avoid the elimination of another specific plater, regardless of that player's team. If the mission is completed within the entire match, the player wins.

Confession

"Unburden your soul and seek redemption: Welcome to Confession." the player picks another player and shows him the team he belongs to.

Dossier

"Knowledge is Power: obtain the Dossier and ontrol the Game!" the player picks a team and two players, and is informed if at least one of the players belongs to that team.

Tip

"Every detail counts: follow the Tip and outsmart your adversaries!" the player is informed of another player's team.

Colleagues

"Trust no one but your colleagues: work together to complete the mission!" the player sees the names of two players belonging to the same team, but not the name of their team.
